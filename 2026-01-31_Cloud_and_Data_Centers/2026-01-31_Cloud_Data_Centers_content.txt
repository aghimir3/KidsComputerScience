How Does Cloud Computing Work?
1
You open an app(YouTube, Drive, etc.)
2
Your request travelsthrough the internet
3
Arrives at adata center
4
Server processesyour request
5
Response sentback to you!
All of this happens in MILLISECONDS!(faster than you can blink)

==================== SLIDE 12 ====================
Local vs. Cloud Storage
LOCAL STORAGE
(On YOUR device)
Your phone's storage
Computer's hard drive
USB flash drives
Works offline
Can be lost/damaged
CLOUD STORAGE
(On remote servers)
Google Drive, iCloud
Accessible anywhere
Backed up automatically
Needs internet
Shared across devices
Best practice: Use BOTH! Cloud for backup, local for speed.

Check Privacy Settings
Control who sees your stuff
