A Complete Year of Computer Science

Three phases that build on each other — from foundations to AI to programming.

Jan – Mar Foundations

Learn how computers and the internet actually work — from the hardware inside your PC to cloud computing, Linux, and command line tools. Students also learn cybersecurity basics to stay safe online.

Apr – Jun Artificial Intelligence ★ Starts April 4

The hottest topic in tech — and your child will understand it. Students learn prompt engineering, work hands-on with ChatGPT, Claude Code, and OpenClaw, build their own AI agents, and explore AI ethics and safety.

Jul – Dec Programming

From writing their first line of code to building a complete software project. Students learn programming fundamentals, developer tools like VS Code and GitHub, logic and problem-solving, and data structures — finishing with a capstone project they can showcase.

Enrollment opens every January, April, and July — join at the start of any phase and build from there.
