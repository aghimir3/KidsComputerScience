# 2026-02-07 Kahoot Questions (Internet Backbones & ISPs)

Q1: What does ISP stand for?
A) Internet Service Provider
B) Internet Safety Program
C) Internal Server Protocol
D) Instant Streaming Platform
Correct: A

Q2: Which company is an example of an ISP?

B) YouTube

D) Google Docs

B) Water
